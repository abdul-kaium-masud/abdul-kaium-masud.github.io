About Me...

I am Minhaz Uddin Ahmed. Bangladesh National University (NU). My name is Minhaz Uddin Ahmed. I obtained my bachelor and masters degree in computer science from Sk. Burhanuddin College, under National University, Bangladesh. After my bachelor in CS. I worked as a software engineer in several software companies in Bangladesh. They are Databiz Inc, Probity Systems Limited, Epsilon Consulting and Development Services. Later, I joined to Bangladesh Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock as a programmer. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD from ITLab, Inha University, South Korea. I was also course instructor in the department of computer engineering. My main research interests are -
   1. Computer vision - Human Action Recognition, Object Tracking.
   2. Machine learning --Active Learning, Deep Learning, Semi-supervised Learning.
